Welcome to Westside Secondary School

Westside Secondary School is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing excellence in ro-school education. Our commitment to academic achievement, personal growth, and community engagement sets us apart.

Our Mission

We strive to create a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically and personally. Our dedicated staff team works tirelessly to ensure every student receives the support and guidance they need to succeed.

Why Choose Westside?

  • Experienced and passionate teaching staff
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering all subjects
  • Supportive pastoral care system
  • Active community engagement
  • Modern teaching methods and resources
